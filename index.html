Chelsey Gravel Designs

I am a UX/UI designer with a passion for user experience design along with UX development and engineering. I love creating, analyzing and front-end coding. As a designer with a background in teaching children, I enjoy learning about people from people. How people interact with technology and how design plays such a large role in a users experience. I create designs to be organized, clean, simple and rewarding for users to experience. I enjoy working on my own with all of the resources available, or with a team for inspiration, feedback and accountability when joining together to meet goals.
